Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download anime episodes?

Simply navigate to the anime page, find the episode you want to download, and click the "Download" button. Your browser will begin downloading the file.

What video quality do you offer?

We offer high-quality video downloads for all our content.

Is this site free to use?

Yes, AnimeStream is completely free to use. We're supported by minimal non-intrusive ads.

How often do you update the content?

We update our library regularly with new episodes and series. Check back frequently for the latest additions.

Can I request an anime to be added?

Absolutely! Send us your requests via email, and we'll do our best to add them to our library.

Is AnimeStream available on mobile devices?

Yes, our website is fully responsive and works well on all mobile devices and tablets.
